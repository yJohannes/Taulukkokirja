Logaritminen derivointi

\begin{align*} y &= x^{3x} \\ \text{Otetaan logaritmi puolittain:} \quad \ln y &= 3x \ln x \\ \text{Differentioidaan puolittain:} \quad \frac{1}{y} \, dy &= \left( 3 \ln x + 3 \right) dx \quad \parallel \cdot y \\ dy &= 3y \left( \ln x + 1 \right) dx \\ \frac{dy}{dx} &= 3y \left( \ln x + 1 \right) \\ \frac{dy}{dx} &= 3 x^{3x} \left( \ln x + 1 \right) \end{align*}
