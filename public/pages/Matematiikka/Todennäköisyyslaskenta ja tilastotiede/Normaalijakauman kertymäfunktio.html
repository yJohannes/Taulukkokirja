\Phi(x)=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}\int_{-\infty}^x\text{e}^{-\frac{t^2}{2}}\text{d}t
\Phi (a)=\text{P}(X\le a),\ \,\Phi (-a)=1-\Phi (a)
