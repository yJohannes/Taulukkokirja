\int{kf(x)\text{d}x}=k\int{f(x)\text{d}x}
\int{(f(x)+g(x))\,\text{d}x}=\int{f(x)\,\text{d}x}+\int{g(x)\,\text{d}x}
\int{{f}'(x)g(x)\,\text{d}x}=f(x)g(x)-\int{{g}'(x)f(x)\,\text{d}x} osittaisintegrointi
\int{{f}'(x)\,{g}'(f(x))\,\text{d}x}=g(f(x))+C funktio ja sisäfunktio
Määrätty integraali
\int_a^{c}{f(x)\text{d}x}=\int_a^{b}{f(x)\text{d}x}+\int_b^{c}{f(x)\text{d}x}
\int_a^{a}{f(x)\text{d}x}=0
\int_a^{b}{f(x)\text{d}x}=-\int_b^{a}{f(x)\text{d}x}
\int_a^{b}{f(x)\text{d}x}\ge \int_a^{b}{g(x)\text{d}x} f(x) \geq g(x) integroimisvälillä
\int_{a}^{b}{{f}'(x)g(x)\text{d}x}=\bigg/_{\!\!\!\!\!a}^bf(x)g(x)-\int_{a}^{b}{{g}'(x)f(x)\text{d}x} osittaisintegrointi
\int_{-a}^af(x)\text{d}x=2\int_0^af(x)\text{d}x jos f(-x)=f(x) (parillinen)
\int_{-a}^{a}{f(x)\text{d}x}=0 jos f(-x)=-f(x) (pariton)
\int _a^{\infty }f\left(x\right)\ \text{d}x=\lim _{t\to \infty }\int _a^tf\left(x\right)\ \text{d}x epäoleellinen integraali
